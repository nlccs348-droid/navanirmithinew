About Us

Navanirmithi Cleaning Company is a wing of Navanirmithi Labour Contract Co-operative Society Ltd. No. PT 348, offering reliable and professional cleaning services. We combine trained staff, eco-friendly practices, and a commitment to quality to keep your spaces spotless. By choosing us, you support a co-operative that creates sustainable jobs while delivering trusted cleaning solutions.

Our Services

Home/Office Cleaning

Comprehensive deep cleaning for your house, apartment, or commercial office space. Choose from one-time or recurring visits.

Well Cleaning

Expert cleaning and disinfection of wells to ensure your groundwater is free of sediment, debris, and contamination.

Interlock Cleaning

Professional high-pressure washing to remove moss, weeds, and dirt from interlock tiles, restoring their original look.

Car Wash@Doorstep

Convenient and thorough car washing service right at your home or office, saving you time and effort.

Water Tank Cleaning

Professional, sanitized, and eco-friendly cleaning methods to ensure your water storage is safe and clean.

Solar Panel Cleaning

Remove dust and grime to maximize your energy efficiency and ensure your investment delivers full power.
